Who we are

A tech enthusiast group for highly-skilled immigrants: those who like to support others, who are already in or are interested in pivoting to tech careers and startups. We will be hosting online and in-person events on a range of tech topics. Looking forward to exploring and networking with immigrants far and wide.

Our story

In 2019, Wunmi Adekanmbi took her first steps into Alberta’s dynamic tech landscape, unaware of the remarkable journey ahead. She discovered a vibrant, innovative community driven by the principle of giving first. Though she felt embraced, she also recognized that she wasn’t explicitly invited.

Despite the warmth and support, Wunmi recognized the need for greater diversity. So, she extended a heartfelt invitation to immigrants like herself — founding Immigrant Techies Alberta.

Our goal

We provide community and support for professional immigrants who desire to pivot to or advance in tech careers and businesses.

Our vision

We are a vibrant, inclusive tech community where immigrant professionals leverage their diverse backgrounds and experiences to make a meaningful impact on the Alberta tech sector. We are the go-to platform for connecting immigrants in tech, facilitating collaborations, and promoting equal opportunities for every member, regardless of their cultural background.
